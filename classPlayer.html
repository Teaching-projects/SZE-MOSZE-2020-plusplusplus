Detailed Description

It defines a RPG attacker Player.

It contains the main data what are describe the Player and all available actions what the player can do or could happen with itself.

The Player can duel with other players. The winner will be the player who has more damage or more health, but important paramter the attack cooldown. With smaller cooldown the player hit the other player faster.

Note
It contains recursive solutions
